Why?!

It is not just an occupation for me; this is a passion project that has been in the making since I was a child and will continue until that creative spark is lost. It's a continual growth of my skills,
but it is also fulfilling to take someone's idea and make it manifest before their eyes, being able to see that character representation of themselves and their interests in a communal game.

While art is subjective, it is also the product of someone's time and skill. However, burgeoning, recent technologies have allowed the possibility of generated artwork to meet someone's inputted specifications,
but the difference between an AI-generated image and a commissioned artist is the uniqueness and authenticity of the product. AI products are aggregated from a wide net of references, resulting in nothing more than a templated reference that cannot legally be fully owned by the creator. An artistic rendition gotten by an artist is both able to be owned by you and is something that is entirely unique to your specifications.
